Nisa Büke Özer, Istanbul, March 2021

Nisa was born in 1997 in Istanbul, Turkey. She grew up as a curious child and a fast learner. She got a full score in her national high school exam and attended Robert College which is a prestigious American high school in Istanbul. Nisa discovered her curiosity for other cultures and passion for developing a global outlook during her time at her international high school. She also played at the school orchestra as a drummer and performed in several gigs. She was the cool girl. During her studies, she attended a part time music academy and managed to get London College of Music certificates for piano. She can now teach music as an certified piano teacher globally.

After graduation, she studied industrial engineering at Koc University, Istanbul. She discovered her love for data analytics when she took her first statistics class. During summers, she did analytics internships at Ford and Deloitte and gained professional insights in the analytics area. She completed her exchange semester at University of Warwick and decided to continue her graduate studies in the UK. Finally, Nisa joined London Business School as a Masters student in Analytics and Management. She is especially looking forward to her Applied Statistics with R course. It is important to emphasize that Nisa is a well rounded person and has a diverse set of hobbies.

Nisa’s favourite hobbies are as following:

  • Playing the drums and the piano
  • Swimming -butterfly strokes are her favourite- and casual kayaking
  • Painting on canvas and charcoal drawing
  • Learning languages
  • Books
